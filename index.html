Thursday - Scorched Pololu A4988

Killed my first Pololu A4988 today. From what I have red, it won't be the last. I messed up the wiring. VMOT and GND were switched, also VDD wasn't connected. The result was a short glow, follwed by a small flame until I switched off the power. Sadly the connected, brand new, Arduino Due got a little bit damaged. The digital pin 13 won't turn off any more and the Arduino gets really hot. Otherwise it works.
